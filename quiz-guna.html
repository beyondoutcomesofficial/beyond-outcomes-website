Bhagavad Gita · Chapter 14 · Three Qualities of Nature

What Is Your Dominant Guna?

The Gita teaches that all of nature — including your mind — is woven from three subtle forces: Sattva, Rajas, and Tamas. They are not personality types but living energies, shifting with your choices, relationships, and inner state. This inquiry reveals which Guna presently dominates your consciousness.

What You Will Discover
  • Sattva — Clarity, luminosity, and harmonious awareness. The guiding force of understanding and inner light.
  • Rajas — Passion, ambition, and restless motion. The energy of desire, drive, and constant becoming.
  • Tamas — Inertia, heaviness, and unconscious patterning. The pull toward comfort, avoidance, and dulled awareness.
